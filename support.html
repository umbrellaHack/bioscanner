(FAQ) Frequently Asked Questions

Using the bracelet:

How does the scanner work?: The scanner uses 3D graphics scanner technology developped by umbrellaHack to be constantly scanning your body and providing the information to your bracelet (Subject to change).

Why does my scanner blink red?: When the scanner is blinking red, it is to notify you of an urgent health concern. Make sure to check the bracelet to see what is happening.
Safety and Security: Who can I share my information with?: While it is possible to share information with anyone, it is recommended to not do so. It is recommended to only share body statistics with licensed healthcare professionals. If you have shared body statistics with a dangerous group, contact us directly by e-mail or phone number.
Resources: How do I access more of my information?: You can find more information about your body by navigating to the mobile app or desktop browser for BioScanner(To change) and selecting what you want to see. How do I choose what my bracelet shows me?: You can select [up to 10 body statistics] to have your bracelet displaying by itself 24/7 through the mobile app or desktop browser for BioScanner(To change).
