About Umbrella

Umbrella Hack is tech company founded on trust by five brilliant engineers from the University of Ottawa. It was founded in 2022 as an idea which came to fruition. It aims at making the life of humanity better by providing the means to maintain good health.

Umbrella Hack is the creator of the infamous Bioscanner which will save the lives of countless people and make the lives of Terrians better.
